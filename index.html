glori.tech

glori.tech is a platform where Minecraft Modification Developers can publish their software and distribute it to their users very easily. We have the Glori API, where developers are given a login and access to the panel, where they can publish their mods with ease. It takes just an upload and a click of a button to publish an update to the entire internet, where their installers or launchers can easily access the files.
